About Us

Blueprint is historic.

The Blueprint has been published annually at the Georgia Institute of Technology for over a century; our first volume was published in 1908. Our student-led staff compiled the major events from the school year covering a broad range of topics, including sports, student organizations, and campus culture.

Sadly, the Blueprint fell into hiatus in 2018, but looks to return this year as the documentarian of student life at Tech, creating a volume of memories to last a lifetime.
